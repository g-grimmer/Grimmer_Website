About

I'm in my first year of PhD study at the University of Strasbourg. The aim of my research is to study the phenomena of river wood and anthropogenic debris in alluvial systems using deep learning and machine learning methods applied to image processing. During my university studies, I acquired a number of skills in the acquisition, management, processing and presentation of spatial data (geomatics). I have specialised in remote sensing and am currently acquiring robust skills in artificial intelligence methods.
